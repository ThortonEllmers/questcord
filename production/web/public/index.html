Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with QuestCord

How do I get started with QuestCord? ‚ñº

Simply click "Add to Discord" above to invite QuestCord to your server, then use /help to see all available commands. The bot will guide you through your first adventure!

What commands does QuestCord have? ‚ñº

Key commands include:

  • /travel - Explore and move between Discord servers
  • /inventory - Manage your items and equipment
  • /market - Trade items with other players
  • /boss - Engage in epic boss battles
  • /stats - Check your health, stamina, and progress
  • /help - View all available commands by category
Is QuestCord free to use? ‚ñº

Yes! QuestCord is completely free with all core features available. We also offer optional premium features for enhanced customization and exclusive content.

How do I get support or report bugs? ‚ñº

Join our support Discord using the "Join Community" button above and create a ticket. Our team responds quickly to help resolve any issues.
